2D Game · Personal Gift Project

Keem HBD Project

A small 2D game I created as a birthday present for my friend. The reward at the end of the game is a Steam Wallet code hidden in the story.

How the idea started

I wanted to create something more meaningful than a simple “Happy Birthday” message. So I turned the celebration into a short game where every level reflects a memory we share — from our first co-op games to late-night Discord calls.

The goal was to keep the gameplay simple but emotional: light platforming, small puzzles, and little details only my friend would recognize.

Design & atmosphere

I used a simple 2D art style with strong lighting and color to focus on mood rather than graphics. Each area represents a different “chapter” of our friendship.

The UI and transitions were kept minimal to let the environment and music carry the emotion — like a personal letter you can walk through.

Ending & hidden present

At the end of the game, instead of a normal “The End” screen, there’s a final room. Solving a small puzzle reveals the Steam Wallet code as part of the environment.

Watching my friend discover the secret was the best part. It reminded me why I love making games.
