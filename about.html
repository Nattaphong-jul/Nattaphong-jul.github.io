My journey into game development

In 2018, my favourite game at that time was Minecraft. One day, my friend and I played a custom map for the first time. It was a mini-game inside Minecraft, and we enjoyed it so much. At that moment, I thought to myself, maybe I can create a game like this too. So I started learning Command Block commands and the Redstone system, and I created many mini-games for my friends to play.

One of my favourite maps I made was a "Find the Button" map in 2019. I loved seeing my friends' reactions when they found the hidden buttons in the weirdest spots ever. It made me realize how much fun it is to create games that bring joy to others. That was the beginning of my game development journey.

Where My Inspiration Began

TheFatRat's music plays a big role in my inspiration. In 2017, I first heard his music from YouTube gaming videos. The electronic beats mixed with orchestral elements created an epic and adventurous atmosphere. The song I liked at the time was "Monody" — the music felt completely different from anything I had heard before. The artwork was stunning, and I tried to look for lore behind the music but found nothing. So I kept listening more and more.

Until one day in 2019, Close To The Sun by TheFatRat & Anjulie was released. It became the best music I had ever heard in my life. Beyond that, the artwork was gorgeous—every part of it felt like it was telling a story. At that moment, I wished there was a game set in a world like that. But I found out nothing like it existed. And I started wondering, what if I make a game in a world like that one day?
